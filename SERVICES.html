Services

The Canine Country Club is your dog’s home away from home. In addition to love and affection, we offer the following services:

Boarding:short-term and long-term boarding for dogs and puppies
Training:private in-home training, group classes, behavior modification
Grooming:dog grooming / washing / bathing / salon services
daycare:Daycare: comprehensive doggie daycare, with an emphasis on *care*

Though we are not a veterinary hospital, we will also ensure that your pet gets any emergency care as needed when they are staying with us. **YOUR DOGS MUST HAVE THE BORDETELLA VACCINE LESS THAN SIX MONTHS (AND NO LESS THAN ONE WEEK) PRIOR TO BOARDING WITH US.
