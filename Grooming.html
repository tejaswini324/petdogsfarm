Grooming

            

Canine Country Club offers full-service grooming by our Groomer, Dee Ratcliff. Dee specializes in hand scissoring, a unique technique that provides quality care and a low stress experience for your dog. Dee has worked with all breeds, offering appearance, health, and safety assurance for your pet. Canine Country Club uses only soap-free shampoos and conditioners specifically formulated to be pH balanced for your dog. Your dog will enjoy being groomed in a spa-like atmosphere here at Canine Country Club. Typically, most dogs take about 3-4 hours to be groomed.

Services offered include:

  • Basic bath, nail clipping, and ear cleaning
  • Medicated baths
  • Breed-specific haircuts
  • Show-quality haircuts
  • Basic bath starts
  • Basic grooming starts

For a complete estimate a consultation with the groomer is required.
