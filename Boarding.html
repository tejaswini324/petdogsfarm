Boarding Accommodation

When you board your dog at Canine Country Club, you can choose from three luxurious accommodations:

Luxury Cabanas

These climate controlled suites are perfect for the ultra pampered pet! With a poolside view, your dog can sit and watch all of the action. Each cabana is themed and decorated which means Fido can relax in a lake, spa, golf, or tennis-themed room. Each dog will have a comfortable bed, color television, and private yard where they can play. And when it gets hot, there is a pool for them to cool down.

Center Court and Front Nine Suites

Our indoor/outdoor climate controlled runs feature cozy dog beds and private sound-dampened suites suitable for relaxing after a long day of fun. Your furry family member will love staying in this upscale suite. Note: Indoor runs available upon request.

Back Nine Suites

Affordable Luxury for your furry friend. Back nine suites include raised bedding, climate controlled building and sunny outdoor runs. These suites are fun for all.
